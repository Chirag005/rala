## üìù Glossary

Quick reference for common terms used throughout documentation:

- **RALA**: Reinforcement-learning Autonomous Life-support Automation
- **VPD**: Vapor Pressure Deficit
- **PPFD**: Photosynthetic Photon Flux Density
- **DLI**: Daily Light Integral
- **PPO**: Proximal Policy Optimization (RL algorithm)
- **CEA**: Controlled Environment Agriculture
- **MRR**: Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **ARR**: Annual Recurring Revenue
- **Digital Twin**: Virtual replica of physical greenhouse
- **Edge Computing**: Local processing at greenhouse site
