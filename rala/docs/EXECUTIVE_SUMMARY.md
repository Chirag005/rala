# RALA - Executive Summary

## One-Line Pitch

**RALA is an AI-powered autonomous operating system that cuts greenhouse energy costs by 30% and boosts yields by 12% through reinforcement learning and digital twin technology.**

## The Problem

Greenhouses face three critical challenges:

1. **Energy Crisis**: Energy is the #1 operating expense (30-40% of costs)
2. **Manual Optimization**: Impossible to optimize thousands of parameters 24/7
3. **Slow ROI**: Traditional ag-tech focuses on yield (seasonal results) vs. energy (immediate savings)

**Market Reality**: $110B global greenhouse market with outdated automation technology

3. **Digital Twin**: Virtual greenhouse for risk-free optimization

## Technology Stack

### Machine Learning

- **Algorithm**: PPO (Proximal Policy Optimization)
- **Framework**: Stable Baselines 3 + PyTorch
- **Deployment**: ONNX Runtime (edge inference)

### Backend

- **Language**: Python 3.11+
- **API**: FastAPI
- **Databases**: InfluxDB (time-series), PostgreSQL, Redis

### Frontend

- **Web**: Nuxt 3 (Vue 3) with custom components
- **Mobile**: Swift (iOS), Kotlin (Android)
- **Visualization**: Custom SVG, D3.js, Three.js

### Edge Computing

- **Hardware**: Industrial IoT gateway (ARM64)
- **Protocols**: Modbus, BACnet, MQTT
- **Architecture**: Offline-first (works without internet)
